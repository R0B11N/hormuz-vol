# War Premium and Geopolitical Price Discovery

## Abstract (Draft)
This paper studies geopolitical price discovery during the 2026 Iran-Israel conflict by combining
prediction-market ceasefire probabilities with option-implied oil distributions from CL and USO.
Using a daily Breeden-Litzenberger framework with SVI interpolation, we recover risk-neutral tails
and compare them to a calibrated Polymarket-implied spike probability. Event-window evidence and
robustness splits characterize when and where each market adjusts first, with special attention to
after-hours intervals and elevated-volatility regimes.

## Limitations Statement (for inclusion)
If the verified event sample is small, Granger and CAR inference have low statistical power.
The paper reports this explicitly and prioritizes transparent diagnostics over over-interpretation.
